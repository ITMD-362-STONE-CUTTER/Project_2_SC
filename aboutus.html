Our Story: Life Carved in Stone

Steinmetz Reuter is a journey that started in 1884, having roots in the passion for natural stone craftsmanship. For over 130 years, we have combined the artistry of an age with extraordinary skills in stone monuments to create legacies. Every order tells some story through the stone—from a memorial to customized art pieces or historical restoration. Behind every stone we carve is the business: our people, our values, and our dedication to heritage.

Legacy: Tradition Cast in Stone

In the beginning of Steinmetz Reuter, it was founded by Fritz Reuter, an artisan with passion, and a keen eye for making artistry an art. From a small workshop in Munich, that soon grew into one of the household names in all the stonemasonry world. Moving onward, the craft never slowed, and our workshop for so long has been a place where tradition never stopped fusing into innovation.

Carving history, preserving memories, and making timeless art; that is what any stone tells. Here at our still living monument to the legacy of Fritz Reuter, generations of artisans have passed through its doors, and here, stones are shaped, not just for their beauty but also revered, for the stories they hold that came with them.

What We Do: Art of Stonemasonry

At Steinmetz Reuter, we have a vast range of services aimed at providing functional and artistic solutions for all our clients. We recognize that every project is unique and must be treated with respect, care, and detail. Here are our specializations:

Custom-made Memorials

We have memorial stones that are not mere markers but expressions of dependency; love, loss, and remembrance must be engraved deep inside. We design with families jointly, nurturing creations reflective of their unique loved ones. Custom engravings, ornamental sculptures—whatever it is, within every memorial, there is a meticulous design to honor who it represents. Each one we carve becomes tribute to a life well lived and multiplied in beauty to essentially create the lasting and beautiful touch.

Restoration & Conservation: Preserving the Past

Restoration is work—in this regard, it certainly has a duty attached to it. We engage in massive rejuvenation of old monuments and works of historical art. Be it inscriptions that have been weathered, repairs to intricate carvings, or any but conservation of original stone worked as petrasit, we feel a lot of pride in making the beauty and history of every piece available for posterity. Thus, we bring history to life through careful craftsmanship; one stone at a time.

The Custom Stone Sculpture

It's not just what we do; it is also what we call art. It's what we strive for in taking custom stone sculptures to the next level. Perhaps you are after an original piece or maybe a statue sculpture for the public space, we can sculpt to your vision. We will work with you to bring your ideas to life using the finest stone.
