Our Story: Life Carved in Stone

Steinmetz Reuter started in 1884, having roots in the passion for natural stone craftsmanship. For over 130 years, we have combined the artistry of an age with extraordinary skills in stone monuments to create legacies. Every order tells some story through the stone from a memorial to customized art pieces or historical restoration. Behind every stone we carve is the business: our people, our values, and our dedication to heritage.

Legacy: Tradition Cast in Stone

In the beginning of Steinmetz Reuter, it was founded by Fritz Reuter, an artisan with passion, and a keen eye for making artistry an art. From a small workshop in Munich soon grew into one of the household names in the stonemasonry world. Over the years, the craft never slowed, and our workshop for so long has been a place where tradition never stopped fusing into innovation.

Carving history, preserving memories, and making timeless art; that is what any stone tells. Here at our living monument to the legacy of Fritz Reuter, generations of artisans have passed through its doors. It is here that stones are shaped, not just for their beauty but also revered for the stories they hold that came with them.

What We Do: Art of Stonemasonry

At Steinmetz Reuter, we have a vast range of services aimed at providing functional and artistic solutions for all our clients. We recognize that every project is unique and must be treated with respect, care, and detail. Here are our specializations:

Custom-made Memorials

We have memorial stones that are not just markers but expressions of dependency where love, loss, and remembrance must be engraved deep inside. We design with families in mind, nurturing creations reflective of their unique loved ones. Custom engravings, ornamental sculptures, or whatever you desire. Within every memorial, there is a meticulous design to honor who it represents. Each one we carve becomes tribute to a life well lived and multiplied in beauty to essentially create the lasting and beautiful touch.

Restoration & Conservation: Preserving the Past

Restoration is work. In this regard, it certainly has a duty attached to it. We engage in massive rejuvenation of old monuments and works of historical art, whether it's weathered inscriptions, repairs to intricate carvings, or any conservation of original stone. We take pride in making the beauty and history of every piece available for posterity. Thus, we bring history to life through careful craftsmanship one stone at a time.

The Custom Stone Sculpture

It's not just what we do, but it is what we call art. It's what we strive for in taking custom stone sculptures to the next level. Perhaps you are after an original piece or maybe a statue sculpture for the public space, we can sculpt to your vision. We will work with you to bring your ideas to life using the finest stone.

Excellence in Every Stone

True craftsmanship comes from a combination of quality, precision, and respect for the material. That is why we have a staff of talented artisans who have trained for years in the dedication to the craft of stonemasonry. Each stone we work with is treated with care and respect, ensuring that our work is truly timeless.

We're not just about building stone monuments; we're creating everlasting memories. We understand you're entrusting us with something incredibly precious when you're working with us, and we treat every project with utter dedication. So much so, that at the end of the day, it is a masterpiece, bending the line between exceeding expectations and honoring your vision.

Our Workshop: Tradition Meets Innovation

Our workshop is a location in which master craftsmanship of old meets new technology. This is where state-of-the-art tools and eyes for perfection join hearts in old-world little Munich. From here, raw stone is transformed into brilliant pieces of art by our top-notch team using state-of-the-art tools and an eye for perfection. In our work, you can be certain that we don't forget our roots, in which we carried out a tradition that transformed us into who we are today.
