Our Legacy

Fritz Reuter Natursteine has been a respected specialist in natural stone sculpture since 1884.

With nearly 130 years of history, we provide mastery in sculpture, gravestones, restoration, and custom work.

Our heritage is based on a legacy of artistry, quality craftsmanship, and close understanding of the material. We remain true to our heritage while introducing new methods and innovation.
