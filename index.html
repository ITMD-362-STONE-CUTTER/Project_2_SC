Master Stonemasonry Reuter

Master Stonemasonry Reuter - Over a century of experience in the area of stoneworks including restoration, custom designs, and monument services. Our concern about precision and craftsmanship guarantees that every project of ours will demonstrate a commitment towards both tradition and excellence. From a simple gravestone to an elaborate stone sculpture or professional restoration, our staff is available to serve you.

What We Offer

A range of services from custom gravestones and memorials to restoration and conservation, stone sculpture and design, interior and exterior stonework, gravestone inscriptions, and accessories.

Everything will be taken care of to the best of our ability while working carefully and professionally, to make the final product beyond beautiful.

Our Legacy

Fritz Reuter Natursteine has earned its place amongst the revered names in natural stone sculpture as early as 1884.

Almost 130 years in history, bringing mastery in sculpture, gravestones, restoration, and custom work.

Our heritage is hand in hand with an artistic legacy, quality craftsmanship, and a close understanding of the material. We maintain the old world while creating modern methods and advancements.
